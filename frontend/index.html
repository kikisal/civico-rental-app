Green Room

Located 27 km from Piazza Duomo and 15 km from Etnaland, Civico 46 Rooms offers air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms in Santa Maria di Licodia. This completely non-smoking property is 25 km from Angelo Massimino Stadium. Rooms at this guest house are equipped with a coffee machine. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Civico 46 Rooms also offer free WiFi. Each room is equipped with bed linen and towels. Guests at Civico 46 Rooms can enjoy a variety of activities in and around Santa Maria di Licodia, including hiking, golfing, and cycling. Villa Bellini is 26 km from this guest house, while Catania Roman Amphitheater is 26 km away. Catania Fontanarossa Airport is 28 km from the property.
